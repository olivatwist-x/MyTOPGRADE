What is Mytopgrade?

Mytopgrade is an easy-to-use, online educational tool to help Secondary schools students learn

complex concepts taught in the Nigerian Secondary schools Curriculum. Parents can take

advantage of this after-school online video lessons to help their children prepare for each

academic session. Mytopgrade has a collection of over 3000 video lessons, carefully taught

and explained by top educational professionals to help students achieve academic excellence.
